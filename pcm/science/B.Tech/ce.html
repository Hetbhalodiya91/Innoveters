Computer Engineering Information

About Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering integrates computer science and electrical engineering to develop computer systems. It covers areas like software development, networking, AI, and cybersecurity. Graduates can work in various tech domains, including IT, embedded systems, and data science.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Engineering is a **4-year** undergraduate program.

Placements

Top colleges in Gujarat maintain strong placement records with tech giants like **Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Infosys, TCS, and Wipro** hiring graduates with attractive salary packages. Placement rates vary by institution and student performance.
